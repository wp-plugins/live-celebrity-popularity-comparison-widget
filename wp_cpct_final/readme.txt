== Description ==

Compare celebrities in a side by side contest where Google's search statistics show who is more popular. 
Not many people know that Google tracks what people are searching for and the volume of those searches. 
It is easy to compare celebrities in a visual way with this graph generator and see who is more popular without resorting to votes. You will be pretty shocked at who is more popular then who, besides being thoroughly entertained. This tool will be something your social audience will appreciate using and playing around with. 
Install it and see your web site's interaction sky rocket!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-cppw.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Widgets (under Appearance) and drag "WP Celebrity Popularity Comparison Widget" to the sidebar
4. Open "Live Celebrity Popularity Comparison Chart Generator Widget" in sidebar and fill in the title, the first celebrity's name and the second celebrity's name.
5. Save and close sidebar widget.
